# Hikvision CCTV Infrastructure: Optimization and Multi-Site Deployment

**Remita (SystemSpecs) | June 2023 - Present | Lagos, Nigeria**

---

## Overview

I inherited an existing Hikvision CCTV setup at Remita that was underoptimized and not fully managed. Over time I overhauled the configuration, expanded coverage, deployed NVRs from scratch at multiple sites, and now own the full lifecycle of CCTV infrastructure across 4 locations.

The result: 18% improvement in storage efficiency, increased available record days, and 99% uptime sustained across all sites.

---

## Scope of Work

### Optimization of Existing Infrastructure

- Audited existing camera coverage and identified blind spots
- Tuned recording settings: resolution, quality, and frame rate balanced against storage capacity
- Configured motion detection and line alert triggers to reduce unnecessary continuous recording
- Set overwrite policies to maximise usable record days within available storage
- Applied configuration changes that produced an 18% improvement in storage efficiency

### NVR Deployment and Configuration

- Reviewed, installed, and configured NVRs from scratch at multiple locations
- Upgraded hard drives to meet storage requirements
- Adopted cameras onto NVRs and configured per-camera settings and outputs
- Configured recording schedules, network connectivity, and system outputs
- Used SADP tool for device discovery and initial configuration
- Used iVMS-4200 for centralised multi-NVR management across all sites

### Coverage Design and Expansion

- Planned camera placement based on site layout, traffic patterns, and blind spot analysis
- Collaborated with vendors on new deployment projects, providing technical input that shaped architecture decisions
- Executed cutovers at 4 locations, managing the transition from old to new configurations without losing continuity
- Provisioned a standalone Hikvision access control unit at a separate office for attendance marking and logging
- Provisioned an additional access control device for event ticket printing

### Ongoing Management

- Handles all troubleshooting across cameras, NVRs, and access control devices
- Manages password reset and device unbind processes for locked Hikvision devices
- Reviews and interprets troubleshooting logs
- Maintains 99% uptime across all sites
